NeuralBridge Product Guide
Version 1.0 — March 2025

Overview
NeuralBridge is a document intelligence platform for teams. Users upload PDFs and text files, then ask questions in natural language and receive answers with citations from the source material.

Company facts
- Founded: 2024
- Headquarters: Singapore
- Supported languages: 50
- Maximum upload size per file: 25 megabytes
- Supported file types: PDF, TXT, Markdown

Core features
1. Workspace collaboration — Teams organize documents in shared workspaces with roles: owner, admin, member, and viewer.
2. Automatic ingestion — Uploaded files are parsed, split into chunks, and indexed for semantic search.
3. Cited answers — Chat responses include references to the exact passages used.
4. Public share links — Any document can be published behind a read-only link with optional expiry.

Pricing plans
| Plan    | Monthly price | Documents included | Queries included |
|---------|---------------|--------------------|------------------|
| Free    | $0            | 10                 | 100              |
| Pro     | $29           | 500                | 5,000            |
| Enterprise | Custom     | Unlimited          | Unlimited        |

Security
- Data is isolated per organization at the database level.
- All traffic is encrypted in transit with TLS 1.3.
- Uploaded files are stored on encrypted volumes.

Support
- Email: support@neuralbridge.example
- Response time on Pro plans: within 24 hours
- Response time on Enterprise plans: within 4 hours

Getting started
1. Create a workspace and invite teammates.
2. Upload a document and wait until the status shows Ready.
3. Open the document chat and ask a question about the content.
